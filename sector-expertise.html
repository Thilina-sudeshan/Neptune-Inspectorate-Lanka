    Agriculture

    Neptune Inspectorate Lanka has an established network of agricultural specialists and we work with accredited laboratories to ensure compliance with contractual terms, offering professional expertise and accurate testing of agricultural products.

    Our agricultural sector expertise includes commodities and produce such as tea, rubber, coconut, spices etc.

    Our inspection services maximize control at every critical point in the supply chain:

    • Pre-shipment inspection
    • Supervision of loading / transshipment / discharge
    • Packing control
    • Monitoring of cargo storage / handling /stowage
    • Silo and warehouse inspections
    • Stock assessment / inventory
    • Tally of goods / weight control
    • Sampling according to international standards
    • Analytical testing by approved laboratories
    • Hold suitability / cleanliness
    • Draft surveys

    We provide Quality and Quantity certificates, ensuring the safety of consignments for exporters/importers. We serve all agricultural market segments, including grains, seeds, fats, vegetable oils, fibers and alternative fuels.

    Our expertise in the Agricultural sector covers the following areas:

    Loading:

    • Verification of vessel holds, trucks, containers and other storage facilities
    • Pre-shipment quality cargo inspection
    • Control and supervision of agricultural products
    • Cargo condition control and verification during pre-shipment
    • Verification of temperature recorders

    Discharging:

    • Tally inspection
    • Quality inspection
    • Condition inspection of of goods
    • Damage survey
    • Inspection of goods at ports, containers, vessel holds and warehouses

    Container and Quantity Supervision:

    • Checking external and internal conditions of the container
    • Verifying ventilation and cooling unit programming if the container travels with a cooling unit
    • Quantity verification by counting pallets and boxes
    • Checking packing list / invoice references
    • Monitoring of temperature (if required)
    • Container sealing

    Minerals and Petrochemicals

    Neptune Inspectorate Lanka serves all segments of the minerals and petrochemicals market, including coal, non-ferrous metals, minerals, ores, steel, fertilizers, cement, industrial minerals and geochemical and metallurgical materials.

    We offer a broad spectrum of value-added solutions to reduce risk, enhance value and maximize returns at each step of the value chain, from simple quality and quantity inspections to complex inspections at production plants.

    Minerals Services:

    • Pre-shipment inspection
    • Supervision of loading / transshipment / discharge
    • Monitoring of cargo storage / handling / stowage
    • Warehouse inspection
    • Tally of packed goods
    • Weight control on scales
    • Sampling according to international standards
    • Analytical testing in certified laboratories
    • Radioactivity testing
    • Draft survey of sea-going vessels, barges and lighters

    Petrochemicals Services:

    • Supervision of oils and chemical products loading and discharging
    • Quality systems management
    • Quality, quantity, packing and branding inspections
    • Sampling and quality inspections
    • Sampling in accordance with international standards for accurate analytical results
    • Bulk oil cargo measurement and blending
    • Tank cleanliness inspections
    • Tank calibration

    Marine

    • On / Off hire and bunker surveys
    • Hull and machinery inspections
    • Used machinery inspections
