    • About Us

    Neptune Inspectorate Lanka Pte Ltd is a privately-owned company specializing in independent supervision services. Neptune Inspectorate Lanka was established in Sri Lanka in 2012 with the objective of providing clients with services that minimize risks throughout the product trade chain, both domestically and internationally.

    We can assist you throughout your production, distribution and trading processes, as well as with the export and import of all types of goods and services. Our team of professionals are highly qualified, possess extensive expertise in supervision procedures, undergo continuous training and are equipped with the latest technological equipment. We conduct inspections for a wide range of goods and services using our highly skilled team. Our supervision services cover a wide range of commodities, products and materials, as well as various areas such as production processes and plants in both the public and private sectors.

    Our certificates are based on comprehensive reports that include details such as quantity, quality, adjustments, safety approvals, specific requirements and general requirement approvals based on professional judgment. The primary objective is to minimize risks for our clients, the entire commercial chain and the final consumers.

    We offer customized solutions to mitigate major trade risks and our comprehensive service packages cover:

    • Weight, quantity and quality in consignments at loading/discharge ports
    • Marine insurance risks on behalf of our clients with top-tier underwriters
    • Re-treatment services at discharge ports due to infestation during transportation
