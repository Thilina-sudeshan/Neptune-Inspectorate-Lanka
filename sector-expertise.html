FACTORY ASSESSMENTS

A factory assessment audits the production capacity and performance of a factory against proven quality principles. Core areas and processes addressed include:

  • Quality Management System
  • Good Manufacturing Practice (Factory Environment Standards)
  • Product Control
  • Process Control
  • Personnel

FACTORY STOCK QUALITY CONTROL

Quality control is vital aspect of stock control – especially as it may affect the safety of the customers or the quality of the finished product. Efficient stock control should incorporate stock tracking and batch tracking. This means being able to trace a particular item backwards or forwards from source to finished product, and identifying the other items in the batch. Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) can be used to store the information about a products or component's manufacturing date, to ensure that it is sold or processed in time.
