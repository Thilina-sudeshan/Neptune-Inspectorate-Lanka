    Loading, Discharging and Weight Control


    • Loading Control

      We manage the entire logistics and operational chain for export cargo, providing comprehensive condition, quantity and weight control for both break-bulk and containerized shipments. This ensures that all operational processes are aligned with the specific requirements of each product type.


    • Discharing Control

      We offer thorough inspections for cosmetic condition, quantity, quality, defects and weight during the unloading of both break-bulk and containerized cargo.


    • Weight Control

      Our weight control services guarantee full compliance with export/import requirements, providing reliable assurance for product shipments.


    Container Condition Inspections


    We offer pre-consolidation services to ensure containers are in suitable physical condition for loading of goods.

    Our container condition inspections are conducted at:

    • Warehouses
    • Shipping Yards
    • Plants
    • Farms
    • Ports

    Traceability


    Our traceability system enables you to monitor and review all cargo information, including detailed updates, authorizations, temperature readings and assessment of other relevant factors.

    The advantagbes of traceability are:

    • Ability to trace through production processes
    • Review and analysis of new developments

    Calibration of Tanks and Gauges


    Our specialised metering services team provides a wide range of high-reliability liquid and gas metering services.

    We offer both onshore and offshore services, utilising automated equipment technology and advanced diagnostic tools, combined with our extensive expertise, to deliver customized solutions for your company.
