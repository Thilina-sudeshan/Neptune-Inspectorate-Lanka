Our Sector Expertise

Agriculture

Neptune Inspectorate Lanka has an established network of agricultural specialists and we work with accredited laboratories to ensure compliance with contractual terms, offering professional expertise and accurate testing of agricultural products.
Our agricultural sector expertise includes commodities and produce such as tea, rubber, coconut, spices etc.

Our inspection services maximize control at every critical point in the supply chain:

  • Pre-shipment inspection
  • Supervision of loading / transshipment / discharge
  • Packing control
  • Monitoring of cargo storage / handling /stowage
  • Silo and warehouse inspections
  • Stock assessment / inventory
  • Tally of goods / weight control
  • Sampling according to international standards
  • Analytical testing by approved laboratories
  • Hold suitability / cleanliness
  • Draft surveys

Loading:

  • Verification of vessel holds, trucks, containers and other storage facilities
  • Pre-shipment quality cargo inspection
  • Control and supervision of agricultural products
  • Cargo condition control and verification during pre-shipment
  • Verification of temperature recorders

Discharging:

  • Tally inspection
  • Quality inspection
  • Condition inspection of goods
  • Damage survey
  • Inspection of goods at ports, containers, vessel holds and warehouses

Container and Quantity Supervision:

  • Checking external and internal conditions of the container
  • Verifying ventilation and cooling unit programming if the container travels with a cooling unit
  • Quantity verification by counting pallets and boxes
  • Checking packing list / invoice references
  • Monitoring of temperature (if required)
  • Container sealing
