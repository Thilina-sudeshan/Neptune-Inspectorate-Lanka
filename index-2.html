    Years of Experience, Qualities of versatility, Professionalism have joined hands to form Neptune Inspectorate, a team which has the caliber, expertise to handle a variety of Survey jobs with competence, ethics and fair play being its legacy.

    The diversity of our personnel and the years exposure and background gives us the platform to follow systems and process in line with outlined SOP’s. Our Master Mariners, Chief Engineers with a long-standing Survey background are able to work on-line on job basis. It gives us the edge and our Principals the opportunity to monitor on line, real time basis, with group chats on line with attending surveyor team to supervise and give spot instructions and guidelines where necessary.

    The 24x7 window presents dynamics and gives optimum benefit to maintain focus, accuracy and the correct level of a virtual presence and participation. It helps secure client satisfaction and support, altaring confidence of continued jobs making it a unique and rewarding experience and a greater sense of achievement on performance levels and results. Our talented team assures our delivery levels.

    Our journey has been that of accolades for jobs undertaken with accuracy in reporting systems backed by photos, video and advanced technology to assist in our task for client satisfaction. Our organization handles a variety of jobs with dedicated professionals who have the technical knowledge compatible with Global Standards.

    Our areas of Marine, Cargo and Insurance Surveys helps to support our valued Principals to deliver uncompromised value in line with expectations. Sri Lanka with its geographical location and four Ports plus a coal discharging point presents itself as a Regional HUB inviting continuous work opportunities, it's on a growth trajectory with its emerging economy boosting capacity and finest level of services offered.
